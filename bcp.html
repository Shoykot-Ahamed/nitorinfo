BCP SOLUTIONS FOR THE MANUFACTURING INDUSTRYInsights on Navigating the New Normal

Build the Factory of the Future with New-age Tech

The manufacturing industry has taken a severe hit due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Plants are closed, the supply chain is disrupted, and equipment maintenance is not being carried out at optimal intervals. The UN estimates the global economic impact of COVID-19 to cost the economy $1 trillion.

At the same time, Industry 4.0 is at our doorstep. The COVID-19 crisis has intensified the need to digitalize, as well as to adopt new technology to build the Factory of the Future. This does not mean, however, that current business is upended and a major rethinking of systems and processes is necessary. Making incremental improvements by adopting new-age technology is a guaranteed way of mitigating the effects of the global crisis and leaping into the digital age. This is already underway in some parts of the manufacturing sector; a survey by the National Association of Manufacturers found that the manufacturing industry in the US is starting to innovate to adapt to this crisis; 67% are re-engineering processes to reflect COVID-19 protocols, 22% are retooling, and 12% are rethinking their mission.

Nitor Infotech, a Select Tier partner of AWS, has created solutions that enable manufacturing organizations to implement new-age tech. Adopting these technologies will revolutionize each organization that seeks to transform their business.
