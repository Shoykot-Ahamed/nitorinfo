Why Professional Services?

Technology is shifting at a fast pace. The requirement for technology solutions to interact in real time is on the surge. To achieve excellence and meet client expectations, organizations must integrate several key operational systems, generate a new revenue stream, design innovative products, and excel at workforce management (WFM).

Whether your organization requires project planning, application development, strategic planning, custom development, or resources to augment your IT staff, you need technology experts who understand technology and how it fits into an organization’s existing infrastructure.

Nitor delivers industry-leading professional services with the help of expert training and a wide array of technology partnerships such as Microsoft, Dell Boomi, 1010 Data, Progress, Logi Analytics, InterSystems, and many more. Following best practices for optimal user engagement, our qualified project managers and tech consultants work directly with customers to design and implement technology solutions that meet their strategic objectives.

On-Field Deployments

Organizations tackle continuous pressure from today’s continually developing business needs, thereby creating the need to integrate with new technologies and adopt new solutions. To start every project on the right track, on-field deployment comes first on the list. An experienced team is required to plan, direct, and coordinate the activities of on-field deployments to ensure that the client’s objectives are accomplished within the agreed period.
